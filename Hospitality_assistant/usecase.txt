 
1. Hospitality

- Reservation Assistance:
 An AI assistant helps customers make and manage their reservations, suggests personalized recommendations based on past preferences,
  and provides information about amenities and services.

- Guest Feedback Analysis: 
The AI analyzes feedback from guests across various platforms, categorizes sentiments,
 and identifies trends to help management improve services.

- Concierge Service: 
The AI acts as a virtual concierge, assisting guests with recommendations for dining, local attractions, and activities,
 based on their preferences and interests.
